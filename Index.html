What is Anime?

Anime is a Japanese style of animation which is mainly aimed for children,teens and adults.Where it has a variety of genres and categories that can satisyf any newcomer such as comedy,action,slice of life and so on.Anime is vast and is a form of entertainment that allows you to explore so much content

A Brief History of Anime!

Anime had made a start up within 1917 at its earliest in Japan,but mainly took a proper turn in the 1960's through the works of artists such Osamu Tezuka.Who was a pioneer of modern anime who was responsible for creating household names such as "Astro Boy","Black Jack" and "Princess Knight".The word "Anime" is a Japanese abbreviation of animation,where most anime series are adapted from Japanese styled comic books called "Manga".
