SAO:LORE

Sword art online (SAO) is a japanese anime/manga within sub genre of fantasy called isekai meaning otherworld/different world. The anime is based in 2016-2022 that centres about a young boy named Kirigaya Kazuto that goes by Kirito in game. In 2016 Kirito is at the age of 8 and is tired of being forced to train in the martial art, kendo. As a result of this his grandfather gives him a beating for quitting, following that kirito's adopted sister, Sugha, takes it upon herself to undergo intense kendo training to avoid the same faith as kirito. With his athletics and martial arts training out of his life Kirito's intrest in computers increases.
